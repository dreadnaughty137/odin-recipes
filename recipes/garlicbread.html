garlic bread

description

“Sounds difficult, but once you get the hang of it, it's actually quite easy. This bread dresses up Italian meals, and it disappears fast! Make two loaves for company!”

ingredients

  • 1 loaf italian bread or 1 loaf french bread, unsliced
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
    • 2 garlic clove, smashed
  • 3 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

steps

  1. Slice loaf, LEAVING CRUST INTACT, into slices. It is important NOT to slice through the bottom. Set aside.
  2. Mix butter with other ingredients.
  3. Take a length of foil, and place loaf lengthwise on the foil. Very gently, spread slices apart, being careful not to break bottom, and spread with butter mixture. When all slices are spread, use any leftover butter to spread the top of the loaf.
  4. Wrap sides of foil up. Leave top UNCOVERED. Twist ends of foil.
  5. Bake on tray for about 20-30 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
