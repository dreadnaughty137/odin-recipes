spaghetti

description

“I looked everywhere for an easy baked spaghetti that did NOT use mushrooms or cream of mushroom soup (husband is allergic). I am happy to say I found it. You could add mushrooms, green pepper, olives, etc. to this dish for added variety, but I like it just the way it is! This makes a great family meal...just add a tossed salad and some garlic bread, and dinner is ready in about an hour.”

ingredients

  • 8 ounces thin spaghetti
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 (26 ounce) can spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups finely shredded mild cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon oregano

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Cook noodles in boiling water; drain. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and garlic over medium heat; drain.
  2. Combine browned ground beef, spaghetti sauce, and oregano.
  3. Spray a casserole dish with cooking spray. Place 1/3 of noodles in bottom of dish.
  4. Top with 1/3 of spaghetti sauce mixture, and 1/3 of cheese. Repeat twice, ending with cheddar cheese.
  5. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
